Growing the Octant community

The three onboarding models produced very different outcomes. The small Raffle cohort showed much stronger downstream engagement than the two larger partner cohorts: 90.9% of recipients were active in Octant v1 during Epochs 7–11, 77.3% migrated to v2, and the group accounted for 98% of all GLM attributed to v2 migration. This suggests that cohort quality, incentive design, and recipient intent mattered more than scale alone.

Migration path from Epoch 10 allocators
How the 279 active E10 allocators moved through the final funnel
Migration funnel from E10: 279 active E10 allocators → 266 remained eligible in E11 → 122 allocated in E11 → 112 migrated to Octant v2.
Drop-off happened before migration, not during it. Of 279 Epoch 10 allocators, 95.3% remained eligible for Epoch 11 — but only 43.7% chose to allocate again. Of those who did allocate, 91.8% went on to migrate to v2, confirming that the committed core transferred almost entirely.
